Creational Design Patterns:
- Concerned with the method of creating objects
- Factory, Abstract Factory, Singleton, Prototype, Builder, Object Pool

Structural Design Patterns:
- Deals with the composition of classes & objects which form larger structures
- Fascade, Bridge, Composite, Decorater, Adapter, Flyweight, Proxy, Filter

Behavioural Design Patterns:
- Deals with responsibility and interaction between objects
- Strategy, Iterator, Mediator, Command, State, Observer, Template, Visitor

JEE Design Patterns:
- Concerned with providing solutions to Java EE based applications
- MVC, Dependency Injection, DAO, Business Delegate, Intercepting, Service Locator, Transfer Object
